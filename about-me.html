About Me

I'm a graphic designer working out of Chesapeake, Virginia. I have recently graduated from Tidewater Community College and am establishing myself in the real world while considering my options for a bachelor's degree. I've always loved creating and arts and crafts but I've never been very good at physical art. So, when I discovered graphic design, I was ecstatic. There's so much about design that I love, but I'm most passionate about motion graphics and logo design, as evident in my portfolio pieces.
